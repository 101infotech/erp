## Tips & Tricks

### Profile Pictures
- Use a clear, professional photo
- Square images work best (will be cropped to circle)
- Minimum recommended size: 200x200 pixels
- Updates instantly - no page refresh needed

### AI Attendance Insights
- Aim for punctuality score above 80%
- Review AI suggestions weekly
- Track your trend over time
- Consistent clock-in times improve your score

### Weekly Feedback
- Be honest - responses are confidential
- Complete on Thursday to avoid Friday rush
- Use complaints section for serious concerns
- Management reviews all feedback weekly

## Troubleshooting

**Profile picture not uploading?**
- Check file size (must be under 2MB)
- Ensure file format is JPEG, PNG, GIF, or WEBP
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache

**AI insights not showing?**
- Insights appear after at least 5 days of attendance data
- Updates every 7 days
- If missing, contact IT support

**Feedback form errors?**
- All fields with * are required
- Text fields need minimum 10 characters
- Radio buttons must have one selection
- Check error messages in red text

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my profile picture multiple times?**
A: Yes, upload a new picture anytime. Old picture is automatically deleted.

**Q: Who can see my attendance insights?**
A: Only you and HR management can view your insights.

**Q: Is my weekly feedback anonymous?**
A: No, it's confidential but linked to your account. However, complaints section is treated with extra privacy.

**Q: What happens if I miss the Friday deadline?**
A: You can still submit, but try to complete on time. Late submissions may be flagged.

**Q: Can I edit feedback after submission?**
A: No, once submitted for the week, it's locked. Plan your responses carefully.

**Q: How is the AI attendance score calculated?**
A: Based on punctuality (40%), consistency (30%), and regularity (30%). Late arrivals and absences lower your score.

# Employee Self-Service Portal - Quick Start Guide

## Overview

The Employee Self-Service Portal provides employees with secure access to their HR information, including attendance records, payroll details, and leave management.

## Accessing the Portal

### Creating Your Account (First-Time Users)

If you don't have an account yet:

1. Navigate to `http://your-domain.com/login`
2. Click the **"Create account"** link below the login button
3. **Important:** Only employees registered in the company HRM system can create accounts
4. Enter your information:
    - **Name**: Your full name (will be auto-filled from HRM if available)
    - **Email**: Must match the email in our HRM records
    - **Password**: Choose a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
    - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
5. Click **"Register"**
6. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to verify your email
7. Check your email inbox for the verification link

**Note:** If an account already exists with your email, you'll be automatically redirected to the password reset page.

### Logging In

1. **Login URL**: Navigate to `http://your-domain.com/login`
2. **Credentials**: Enter your email and password
3. **Remember Me**: Check this box to stay logged in (optional)
4. Click **"Log In"**
5. **First Login**: You may be required to verify your email address

### Forgot Password?

If you forgot your password:

1. Click **"Forgot password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **"Email Password Reset Link"**
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Follow the instructions to set a new password

## Dashboard Features

After logging in, you'll see your personal dashboard with:

-   **Attendance Summary**: Current month attendance statistics
-   **Leave Balance**: Available leave days for each type
-   **Last Payment**: Your most recent salary payment
-   **Recent Activity**: Latest attendance records and pending leave requests

## Navigation

The portal has four main sections accessible from the top navigation bar:

### 1. My Dashboard

Your central hub showing key information at a glance

### 2. Attendance

-   Filter by date range
-   See hours worked, payroll hours, and overtime

### 3. Payroll

-   View salary history
-   See detailed breakdown of each payslip

### 4. Leave

## How to Request Leave

### Step 1: Check Your Balance

Before requesting leave, check your available balance on the Leave page:

-   **Sick Leave**: 12 days per year
-   **Casual Leave**: 10 days per year
-   **Annual Leave**: 15 days per year
-   **Unpaid Leave**: No limit

### Step 2: Submit Request

1. Click **Request Leave** button
2. Select leave type from dropdown
3. Choose start and end dates
4. Provide a reason (required)
5. Click **Submit Leave Request**

### Step 3: Track Status

-   Your request will appear in the Leave Requests table
-   Status will show as:
    -   **Pending**: Awaiting manager approval
    -   **Approved**: Leave has been approved and deducted from balance
    -   **Rejected**: Request was denied (reason will be shown)
    -   **Cancelled**: You cancelled the request

### Step 4: Cancel if Needed

-   Only **pending** requests can be cancelled
-   Click the cancel icon (Ã—) next to the request
-   Confirm cancellation

## Understanding Your Payslip

### Earnings Section

-   **Base Salary**: Your monthly base salary
-   **Bonus** (if applicable): Performance or other bonuses
-   **Overtime Pay** (if applicable): Additional pay for overtime hours
-   **Gross Salary**: Total earnings before deductions

### Deductions Section

-   **Income Tax**: Calculated based on Nepal's progressive tax slabs
-   **SSF (Social Security Fund)**: Employee contribution
-   **Other Deductions**: Any additional deductions
-   **Total Deductions**: Sum of all deductions

### Net Salary

Your final take-home pay after all deductions

### Tax Breakdown

View detailed breakdown of how your income tax is calculated based on Nepal's tax slabs:

-   Up to NPR 500,000: 1%
-   NPR 500,001 - 700,000: 10%
-   NPR 700,001 - 1,000,000: 20%
-   NPR 1,000,001 - 2,000,000: 30%
-   NPR 2,000,001 - 5,000,000: 36%
-   Above NPR 5,000,000: 39%

## Viewing Attendance Records

### Monthly View (Default)

-   Shows current month's attendance automatically
-   See total days present, hours worked, and average hours

### Custom Date Range

1. Use the date filter form
2. Select **Start Date** and **End Date**
3. Click **Filter**
4. View attendance for the selected period

### Understanding Attendance Status

-   **Full Day**: 8+ hours worked
-   **Partial**: Less than 8 hours worked
-   **Absent**: 0 hours worked

### Attendance Fields

-   **Tracked Hours**: Total hours tracked in system
-   **Payroll Hours**: Hours counted for payroll (excludes breaks, etc.)
-   **Overtime**: Hours worked beyond standard hours

## Downloading Payslips

1. Go to **Payroll** section
2. Find the payroll record you want
3. Click on the record to view details
4. Click **Download Payslip PDF** button
5. PDF will download to your device
6. Save for your records

**Note**: Only payrolls that have been processed and approved will have downloadable PDFs.

## Important Information

### Leave Request Guidelines

-   Submit leave requests at least 24 hours in advance
-   Requests are reviewed by your manager/HR
-   You'll see the status update in your portal
-   Approved leave is automatically deducted from your balance
-   You cannot request more leave than your available balance (except unpaid leave)

### Attendance Tracking

-   Attendance is synced from Jibble time tracking system
-   Records are updated daily (usually overnight)
-   If you see discrepancies, contact HR
-   Overtime is automatically calculated based on your work hours

### Payroll Schedule

-   Payroll is typically processed monthly
-   You'll receive notification when new payslip is available
-   PDFs are generated automatically
-   Contact HR if you have questions about your payslip

### Security Best Practices

-   Keep your password secure
-   Don't share your login credentials
-   Log out when using shared computers
-   Report any suspicious activity to IT/HR

## Common Questions

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

### Q: My attendance hours look wrong. What should I do?

A: Contact your manager or HR department. They can review and make corrections if needed.

### Q: Can I cancel an approved leave request?

A: No, only pending requests can be cancelled. Contact your manager to discuss approved leaves.

### Q: When will I see my latest salary in the portal?

A: Payroll records are added after processing is complete, typically within 1-2 days of payday.

### Q: I don't see a download button for my payslip. Why?

A: PDFs are generated only for approved and paid payroll records. Contact HR if you need assistance.

### Q: Can I request leave for past dates?

A: No, the system only allows future-dated leave requests. Contact HR for exceptional cases.

### Q: What happens if I have insufficient leave balance?

A: The system will prevent you from submitting the request and show an error. You can request unpaid leave instead.

### Q: How do I update my personal information?

A: Contact HR to update personal details like phone number, address, or emergency contacts.
